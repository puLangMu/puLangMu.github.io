aboutMe

我为什么写这个网站

一部分原因是想要记录自己读一些人文社科书籍的读后感。读这些书需要写一些感受来汇总，凝练自己的思考，而非走马观花。

同时，想要记录一下自己的一些技术经验，掌握的技能等等，应该不会写什么细节或者数学推导，主要是一种思维方式吧。

并且，分享一些在zju课程的学习经验，学习资源等等，方便其他人使用。

顺便锻炼下英文翻译？

网站结构

目前想的是分为读书分享，技术交流，课程经验，生活杂事这几个方面，可以在分类栏目查看。

网站名的由来

plum的中文是李子，而其结尾音m也和mu有关，所以用普朗睦或者plum作为用户名。





English Translation

Why I Created this Website

One of the reasons is to document my reflections after reading books in humanities and social sciences. Writing down my thoughts helps me condolidate and distill my ideas, rather tham just skimming through the content.

Meanwhile, I want to share my experiences twith various technical skills and engineering practices. While I won’t go into the detailed steps or mathematical derivations, I'll focus more on the thought processes and approaches.

I also plan to share my experiences with courses and study resources from my time at ZJU, making it easier for others to benefit from them.

website structure

The website is organized into four main sections: Book Reviews, Technical Discussions, Course Experiences, and Daily Musings. You can explore these categories in the navigation bar.

the website'name deriviation

The name "plum" is a play on my own name. In Chinese, “plum” is "lizi," which has a connection to my name. Also, the "m" at the end sounds similar to "Mu," which makes it a fitting username.
