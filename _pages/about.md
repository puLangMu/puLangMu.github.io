I am an undergraduate student in Electronic Science and Technology at Zhejiang University, with a minor in the Advanced Class of Engineering Education through Chu Kochen Honors College.

My research interests lie at the intersection of 3D vision, simulation, and physically grounded generative models. I am especially interested in simulation-ready 3D scene reconstruction, physically interactive scene representations, physics-informed world models, and embodied AI simulation.

A recurring theme in my work is to make visual representations useful beyond rendering. I care about whether reconstructed scenes can be exported, simulated, interacted with, and used by downstream agents or physical systems.

**Research interests:** 3D scene reconstruction, mesh-based scene representations, 3D Gaussian splatting, embodied AI simulation, generative visual models, computational lithography, and physics-informed world models.
