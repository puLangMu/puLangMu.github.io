LithoDreamer models multi-stage computational lithography as a physics-aware state evolution process. The framework couples a policy model and predictive model into a generative-feedback loop, allowing the optimization process to incorporate physical constraints rather than relying only on unconstrained visual generation.

My work focused on core training code and experimental pipelines. I helped introduce a physical latent-space prior approximation to constrain the generation space, and contributed to main experiments and ablation studies that evaluate optimization stability, convergence speed, and final imaging quality.
