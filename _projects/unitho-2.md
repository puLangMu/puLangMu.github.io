Unitho 2.0 is a unified image-translation framework for computational lithography tasks such as mask optimization and high-precision pattern generation. It uses Vision Transformer-based modeling, mixture-of-experts components, and geometric constraints based on signed distance fields.

My work included PyTorch training, evaluation, and comparison pipelines for multiple image generation models on mask optimization tasks, with an emphasis on understanding performance differences and bottlenecks under complex layout patterns.
